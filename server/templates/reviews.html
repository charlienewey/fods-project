Rating Trends

This chart shows averaged review scores (0-100) per wine vintage vs price, aggregated over region. Interestingly, there appears to be a very clear exponential relationship between review score and price - that is, a higher review score causes the price of a bottle of wine to increase exponentially. This is such a strong relationship that a linear regression has been fitted to the wines from each region (note the x-axis is a logarithmic scale). The link between review score and price is so strong that the region a wine was grown in can be effectively determined simply with the price and review score.
