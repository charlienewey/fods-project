Precipitation Trends (Bordeaux)

These charts represent approximately 10 years of precipitation and humidity data across various regions of France. Perhaps notably, there appears to be no significant link between medium-term weather trends and price - for example, in Bordeaux, although there appears to be a faint downward trend in annual rainfall, there appears ot be no discernible effect on wine prices (a slight upward trend perhaps, but this is largely due to the strengthening of the global economy after the 2008 sub-prime mortgage crisis.
