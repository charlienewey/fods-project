Comparing the state of the global economy (using the MSCI world stock index as a proxy) to the price of wine from individual regions of France. Wine prices seem to be somewhat unaffected by the 2008 sub-prime mortgage crisis, but instead seem to stagnate for approximately two years and then recover slowly. Wine prices seem to remain largely unmoved by short-term fluctuations in the market, only displaying change at more significant and long-term market movements.
